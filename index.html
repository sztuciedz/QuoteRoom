FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this a binding quote?
No. This tool produces an estimation quote for indicative purposes only. It is not a formal offer or contract. The figures are based on typical material and labour costs and are intended to give you a realistic ballpark figure. A precise, binding quote will be provided by our team following an on-site visit and full assessment of your project.
Do prices include labour?
Yes — every item in the estimator already includes the cost of materials and installation labour. There are no hidden charges. The price you see is the all-in cost for that element of the renovation.
How long does a bathroom renovation take?
A typical bathroom of 4–8 m² takes 2–4 weeks. The exact timeline depends on the scope of work, chosen materials and team availability. We will provide a detailed schedule once the project is confirmed.
Do I need to register?
No. The estimator is completely free and requires no account. We only ask for your contact details at the very end, if you'd like us to follow up with a formal visit.
What areas do you cover?
We operate across the country. Get in touch and we'll confirm availability in your area.
